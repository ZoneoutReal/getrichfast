SnipKey Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 16, 2026

The short version

SnipKey collects nothing. Your snippets, settings, and usage counts are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted anywhere.

Data storage

  • All snippets, settings, and usage statistics are stored in Chrome's local extension storage (chrome.storage.local) on your device.
  • SnipKey operates no servers, requires no account, and includes no analytics, telemetry, or tracking of any kind.
  • You can export all of your data as JSON, or remove it completely by uninstalling the extension.

Page access

SnipKey's content script runs on web pages so it can detect shortcuts as you type in text fields and replace them with your snippet text. This happens entirely on your device. SnipKey does not read page content beyond the text field being typed in, and it does not transmit page content, keystrokes, or any other data off your device. Password fields are ignored entirely.

Payments (Pro upgrade)

The optional Pro upgrade is processed by ExtensionPay and Stripe. When you purchase, ExtensionPay handles your email address and payment status; card details are handled by Stripe. SnipKey never sees or stores your payment information. See the ExtensionPay privacy policy and the Stripe privacy policy.
