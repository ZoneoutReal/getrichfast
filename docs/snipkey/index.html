Questions, answered.

Where is my data stored?

On your device, in Chrome's local extension storage. SnipKey has no servers, no account system, and no analytics. Export your snippets to JSON any time.

Why does it need access to the pages I visit?

Expansion has to happen where you type, so SnipKey's content script runs on pages to watch for your shortcuts in text fields. It reads only the field you're typing in, replaces shortcuts locally, and transmits nothing.

Is Pro really one-time?

Yes. $15 once, unlimited snippets forever, all updates included. Payments are processed by Stripe via ExtensionPay; we never see your card details.

Does it work in Gmail?

Yes — SnipKey supports rich editors (contenteditable), including Gmail compose, plus every normal input and textarea on the web.

What are the dynamic placeholders?

{date} and {time} insert the current date/time, {date+7} inserts a date N days out, and {cursor} controls where your caret lands after expansion.
