Questions, answered.

Where do my screenshots go?

Nowhere. They're captured and edited entirely on your device. SnipShot has no servers, no account system, and no analytics — there is nowhere for your images to go.

Is Pro really one-time?

Yes. $15 once, all Pro tools forever, every future update included. Payments are processed by Stripe via ExtensionPay; we never see your card details.

Can it capture full scrolling pages?

v1 captures the visible part of the page — full-page capture is on the roadmap. For most annotation jobs (bug reports, tutorials, feedback) the visible viewport plus crop is exactly what you want.

Why can't it capture some pages?

Chrome blocks all extensions from capturing special pages like chrome:// settings and the Web Store itself. Every normal website works.
