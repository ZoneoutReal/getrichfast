SnipShot Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 16, 2026

The short version

SnipShot collects nothing. Your screenshots are captured, edited, and exported entirely on your device — they are never uploaded anywhere.

Screenshots

  • A capture happens only when you click the SnipShot icon or press its keyboard shortcut (Chrome's activeTab permission).
  • The captured image is held in Chrome's local extension storage just long enough to open in the editor, and is overwritten by the next capture. It is never transmitted.
  • Exporting means downloading a file to your computer or copying to your clipboard — both local operations.

Data collection

SnipShot operates no servers and includes no analytics, telemetry, or tracking of any kind. It cannot see your browsing, and it has no access to any page except the single capture you explicitly trigger.

Payments (Pro upgrade)

The optional Pro upgrade is processed by ExtensionPay and Stripe. ExtensionPay handles your email address and payment status; card details are handled by Stripe. SnipShot never sees or stores your payment information. See the ExtensionPay privacy policy and the Stripe privacy policy.
