CopyMark Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 17, 2026

The short version

CopyMark collects nothing. Pages are converted to Markdown entirely on your device and the result goes straight to your clipboard — nothing is recorded or transmitted anywhere.

How it works

  • CopyMark acts only when you explicitly invoke it — via its toolbar button, keyboard shortcut, or context menu entry (Chrome's activeTab permission). It has no access to any page otherwise.
  • The converter runs inside the page, turns your selection (or the article, for Pro full-page clips) into Markdown locally, and writes it to your clipboard.
  • The optional "all tabs" feature reads tab titles/URLs at the moment you click it, builds the list, and forgets them. CopyMark stores nothing about the pages you visit.

Data collection

CopyMark operates no servers and includes no analytics, telemetry, or tracking of any kind. Your settings (Markdown style, front-matter template) are stored locally in Chrome's extension storage and go nowhere.

Payments (Pro upgrade)

The optional Pro upgrade is processed by ExtensionPay and Stripe. ExtensionPay handles your email address and payment status; card details are handled by Stripe. CopyMark never sees or stores your payment information. See the ExtensionPay privacy policy and the Stripe privacy policy.
