MockFill Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 17, 2026

The short version

MockFill collects nothing. All fake data is generated on your device, filled into the page you're on, and never recorded, read back, or transmitted anywhere.

How it works

  • MockFill acts only when you explicitly invoke it — via its toolbar button, keyboard shortcut, or context menu entry (Chrome's activeTab permission). It has no access to any page otherwise.
  • The fill routine runs inside the page, generates data locally, writes it into the form fields, and reports back only a count of how many fields were filled.
  • MockFill never reads existing form values, never submits forms, and never stores anything about the pages you visit.

Data collection

MockFill operates no servers and includes no analytics, telemetry, or tracking of any kind. Your settings (email domain, custom rules, seed) are stored locally in Chrome's extension storage and go nowhere.

Payments (Pro upgrade)

The optional Pro upgrade is processed by ExtensionPay and Stripe. ExtensionPay handles your email address and payment status; card details are handled by Stripe. MockFill never sees or stores your payment information. See the ExtensionPay privacy policy and the Stripe privacy policy.
