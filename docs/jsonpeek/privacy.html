JSONPeek Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 17, 2026

The short version

JSONPeek collects nothing. JSON is parsed, searched, queried and diffed entirely inside the viewer tab on your device — nothing is recorded or transmitted anywhere.

How it works

  • JSONPeek reads a page only when you explicitly invoke it there — via its toolbar icon or keyboard shortcut (Chrome's activeTab permission). It has no access to any page otherwise.
  • The grabbed JSON text is held in Chrome's local extension storage just long enough to open in the viewer, and is overwritten by the next grab. It is never transmitted.
  • Pasted text and dropped files are read locally in the viewer tab and never stored beyond it.

Data collection

JSONPeek operates no servers and includes no analytics, telemetry, or tracking of any kind. Your preferences are stored locally in Chrome's extension storage and go nowhere.

Payments (Pro upgrade)

The optional Pro upgrade is processed by ExtensionPay and Stripe. ExtensionPay handles your email address and payment status; card details are handled by Stripe. JSONPeek never sees or stores your payment information. See the ExtensionPay privacy policy and the Stripe privacy policy.
